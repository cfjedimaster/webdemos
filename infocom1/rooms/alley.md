It is much cooler here in the shadows of the alleyway, but the smell is distinctly worse. Piles of scrap metal and discarded droid parts litter the ground. 

In the corner, you see a glowing red object that looks dangerously like a thermal detonator, right next to a perfectly good fish that someone threw away. Typical Mos Eisley.

The spaceport lies to the **south**.
