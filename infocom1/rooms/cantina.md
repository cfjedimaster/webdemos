**This place is loud.** 

The air is thick with smoke, the smell of cheap fuel, and spilled beverages. A band of hairless alien creatures is playing a catchy tune in the corner. Various rough-looking patrons are nursing their drinks. 

A bartender with a cybernetic eye glares at you. "We don't serve your kind here," he grunts, but then he notices you're a cat and immediately softens. "Wait, who's a good kitty? We got some milk if you want it."

The only exit is back **west** into the glaring suns of the spaceport.
