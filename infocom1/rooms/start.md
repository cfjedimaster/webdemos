You are standing in a dusty spaceport on a desert planet. The twin suns are beating down on you, making your fur uncomfortably hot. 

To the **east**, you hear the unmistakable sound of a bouncy, upbeat band playing in what looks like a rough-and-tumble cantina. 
To the **north**, there is a shady-looking alleyway.

A rusty astromech droid putters past, completely ignoring you.
